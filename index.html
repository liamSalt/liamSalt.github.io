Teacher Candidate , University of Ottawa

Hello!

I am a Teacher Candidate at the University of Ottawa, located in Ottawa, Ontario. My goal is to work as a Grade 7-12 Math/Physics teacher. Before starting my career as a Teacher, I previously worked in post-secondary education, specifically I worked as a Course Coordinator in the department of Computer Science at UBC. My previous educational background culminated in an MSc. in Mathematics from Simon Fraser University in Burnaby, British Columbia, where I was supervised by Professor Petr Lisonek. My thesis research focused on constructing novel Kochen–Specker pairs using Hadamard matrices. I successfully defended my thesis in August of 2023. I previously completed my undergraduate studies at Queen's University in Kingston, Ontario, majoring in Math, but I'm orginally from Windsor, Ontario. I enjoy watching movies, playing music, playing GeoGuessr, and making and solving fun puzzles.
