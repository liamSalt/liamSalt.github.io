My Thesis Research

My Master's thesis focused on an area of Mathematics called Algebraic Combinatorics and how it can be applied to Quantum Information Theory. Specifically, I studied new mathematical approaches to demonstrating the necessity of Contextuality in any model for quantum mechanics that features Hidden Variables. This necessity is known as the Kochen–Specker Theorem. In brief, I employed methods from the toolbox of algebraic combinatorics to find new proofs of this theorem.
