First Practicum Experience

Longfields—Davidson Heights Secondary School

I spent my second practicum at Longfields—Davidson Heights Secondary School in Barrhaven. I worked in the Math department with my Associate Teacher, Matt Brown. I was there from the first to last day of the semester, teaching Advanced Functions (MHF4U) and Data Management (MDM4U). This school has a great community and I truly felt like part of the department. Below are a select few pictures and experiences from my time at LDHSS.
